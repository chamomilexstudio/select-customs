How to Order

Ordering your custom model is straightforward. Here's how it works.

01

Browse the Gallery

Look through the photos for ideas and inspiration. We can replicate any department's markings, colors, and livery.

02

Send Us a Message

Use the contact form below. Tell us the department, vehicle type, and share any reference photos you have.

03

Deposit via PayPal

Once we agree on a price, a deposit is collected securely through PayPal. Balance is due when the model is complete.

04

Shipped to Your Door

Your finished model is carefully packaged and shipped anywhere in the United States — straight from Hilliard, Ohio.
